Floor Guidelines & Safety Policies

Outdoor dirt and moisture damage our vulcanized rubber lifting decks and cable pulley corridors. Members are strictly required to carry a separate pair of clean indoor training shoes. Outdoor shoes must be placed in changing room lockers prior to entering the training floor.
To maintain pristine cleanliness standards, please wipe down benches, handles, and leather paddings immediately after use. Disinfectant wipes are stationed throughout all training pillars. Barbell shafts should be brushed clean of chalk following heavy compound sets.
Elite Annual members are provided daily-use numeric lockers. VIP Platinum members receive dedicated permanent lockers with secure keycodes. Towels and premium grooming accessories are stocked inside changing suites at all times.
To ensure privacy and thorough cleaning protocols between sessions, steam lounges and cold plunge tubs must be booked at least 1 hour in advance. You can reserve your recovery times at the front desk or via WhatsApp.
